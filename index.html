Features

Everything You Need to Run Payroll

From attendance tracking to compliance automation, PrimeHR handles it all. Explore our comprehensive suite of features designed for modern businesses.

Payroll Management

Process salaries with precision. Configure salary components, run multiple payroll cycles, and generate payslips automatically.

  • Flexible salary structure configuration
  • Multiple payroll runs in the same month
  • Automated payslip generation & distribution
  • LOP adjustments & manual overrides
  • Salary arrears & custom deductions
  • Bank transfer statements

Attendance Management

Track employee attendance effortlessly with multiple methods including biometric, mobile, and web check-ins.

  • Biometric, mobile & web attendance
  • Configurable working hours & days
  • Attendance regularization with approval
  • Visual attendance calendar
  • Monthly attendance matrix & summary

Leave Management

Create unlimited leave types with flexible policies. Handle sandwich rules, carry-forwards, and multi-level approvals.

  • Unlimited leave types (CL, SL, EL, Comp-Off)
  • Configurable carry-forward rules
  • Sandwich leave policy support
  • Multi-level approval workflows
  • Email notifications & balance tracking

Statutory Compliance

Stay 100% compliant with automatic calculations for PF, ESI, PT, TDS, and Gratuity. Support for both old and new tax regimes.

  • Auto Professional Tax by state
  • PF & ESI contribution calculations
  • TDS auto-calculation (Old & New regime)
  • Gratuity eligibility tracking
  • Auto-apply statutory deductions

HR & Employee Management

Centralized employee directory with complete lifecycle management from onboarding to exit.

  • Bulk employee invitations (Excel upload)
  • Salary structure & revision management
  • Tax declarations & bank details
  • Exit process initiation
  • HR & Salary registers

Tally Integration

Seamless sync with Tally for automated payroll entries and centralized expense management system.

  • Auto-setup for Tally connection
  • Employee data import/export
  • Auto-sync payroll entries
  • Company expense management
