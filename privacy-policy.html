This Privacy Policy describes how Techstuff Cloud Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the PrimeHR mobile application ("App").

1 Who This App Is For

PrimeHR is an Employee Self-Service (ESS) portal available exclusively to employees of organisations that use the PrimeHR HRMS platform. Access requires valid company credentials issued by your employer.

2 Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Login credentials — email address and password used to authenticate your account
  • Leave requests — leave type, dates, reason, and day type selections submitted through the App

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location data — your device's GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) are collected at the time of each attendance check-in and check-out. Location is only accessed when you actively tap the Check In or Check Out button. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Device information — device type and operating system, used to identify the mode of attendance log (recorded as "mobile")
  • Attendance logs — timestamps of check-in and check-out events

2.3 Information From Your Employer's System

The App retrieves the following data from your organisation's HR system:

  • Your employee profile (name, employee code, designation, department)
  • Attendance records and calendar
  • Leave history and balances
  • Payslips and salary information
  • Organisation settings, holidays, and working calendar

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide access to the App
  • Record attendance check-in and check-out with location data
  • Display your HR data (attendance, leave, payslips, profile)
  • Enable leave application and management
  • Show team information such as who is on leave, upcoming birthdays and anniversaries

4 Location Data

Location access is used only for attendance check-in and check-out.
  • Location is requested at the moment you tap Check In or Check Out
  • Coordinates are transmitted to your organisation's HR system to record the attendance log
  • We do not store location data on our servers independently of the attendance record
  • We do not collect location data in the background or when the App is not in use
  • If you deny location permission, check-in and check-out will still function without location data

5 Data Storage and Security

  • Your authentication token is stored securely on your device using the platform's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • All data transmitted between the App and the server is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS)
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties outside of your organisation's HR system

6 Data Retention

Data is retained in accordance with your organisation's HR data policies. You may contact your HR department or system administrator for information on data retention and deletion.

7 Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Expo (by Expo Inc.) — application framework and over-the-air update delivery
  • Firebase (by Google LLC) — used for push notifications (future feature)

These services have their own privacy policies and data practices.

8 Children's Privacy

PrimeHR is intended for use by employed adults only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18.

9 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

For any such requests, please contact your organisation's HR department or reach us at the address below.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
